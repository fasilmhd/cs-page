Department of Chemistry

Profile

The Department of Chemistry was established in 1991 and offers undergraduate programs in Chemistry and Industrial Chemistry.

Academic Information

Chemistry is a core science with critical application in several sectors. The department provides foundational and advanced knowledge in the subject.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a leader in education and research in the domain of Industrial Chemistry.

  • Equip students with essential skills for Chemistry practice
  • Deliver quality education and foster critical and analytical thinking
  • Encourage students toward higher education and research
