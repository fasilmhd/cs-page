Profile

  • The Department of Biotechnology was established in the year 1997 under the faculty of Science in Government College, Kariavattom, Trivandrum.
  • The Department offers a career related first degree programme in Biotechnology named B.Sc. Biotechnology Multimajor which is unique in having three core papers- Biotechnology,Chemistry and either Botany or Zoology.
  • This subject is heavily demanded and has diverse job prospects in the areas of research and development (R&D) and the expertise is quite useful in the Industrial and Medical fields.

Academic Programme

  • The Department offers BSc Biotechnology (Core) Course and Biotechnology as part of the core course of BSc Biotechnology.
  • It follows the syllabus and course pattern prescribed by the University of Kerala.

Vision

  • The vision is to produce human resource with a potential to innovate, invent and disseminate knowledge on life science for the benefit of humanity and environment.

Mission

  • To initiate faculty development programmes
  • To evolve into an active platform for biotechnology education, training and research at the interphase of multiple disciplines.
  • To provide quality education with a strong foundation on basic concepts of Biotechnology that transform students globally competent.
  • To provide our nation Biotech professionals who are able to implement scientific principles in needed sectors.
  • To update course curriculum regularly to cater the needs of academia and industry.
  • To create opportunities for students to engage in multidisciplinary research.
  • To start PG and M.Phil programmes and to establish a research department.
  • To graduate students who can compete, with advantage, within the relevant academic and commercial markets in the world.
  • To develop infrastructure and instrumentation facilities sufficiently to attain these missions.
  • To organize Conferences, Symposia and Workshops to equip and refresh students and faculty on the recent trends and advances of the subject

Goals

  • To inculcate scientific attitude and research interest in students.
  • To provide quality education that lays a strong foundation on the very basic aspects of the rapidly developing subject- Biotechnology.
